Online Training Courses

In this competitive job market, it's better to have knowledge of some latest technologies, which will increase your chances of getting placed in reputed companies. Join EdUNatioN Online Training program (conducted through Live Classes) to learn some of these technologies. All the courses will cover the Topic from beginning to intermediate level. The Topic and Course Content of the Training has been designed in such a manner that acquired skills can be used throughout the career. And the Course Fee is much lower than offline Classroom Training, so that anyone can afford it easily.

We will be using different tools, like Zoom (for live classes), Whatsapp (for quick support), Google Classroom (for assignments) and Youtube (for class recordings). Overall it will be similar to classroom based training experience or may be better than that.
